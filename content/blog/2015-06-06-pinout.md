Seems easy:

* pin 4 &raquo; 12 Volt (PWRSPLY)
* pin 17 &raquo; 12 Volt (KEYSENSE)
* pin 16 &raquo; K-Line (SERIAL_K)
* pin 34 or pin 10 or heatsink &raquo; ground
